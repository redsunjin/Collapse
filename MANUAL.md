# Reader's Guide (독자 가이드 - 관측 매뉴얼: 붕괴)

## [한글 요약 / Korean Summary]
"Quantum Narratives"의 세계에 오신 것을 환영합니다. 이 문서는 독자들이 어떻게 이 양자 소설을 읽고, 상호작용하며, 새로운 현실을 관측할 수 있는지 안내합니다.
- **조작법**: 텍스트를 읽고 하단의 '관측 버튼'을 클릭하여 이야기를 붕괴(Collapse)시킵니다.
- **상태 확인**: 상단의 상태 표시줄을 통해 현재 이야기가 중첩(Superposition) 상태인지, 아니면 확정된 현실(Collapsed)인지 확인하세요.
- **참여**: 깃허브 이슈(Issues)를 통해 당신의 관측 결과를 공유하거나, 제안(PR)을 통해 새로운 평행 세계를 제안할 수 있습니다.

## [Operating Instructions / 영문 상세 내용]

### 1. The Act of Observation
In this universe, text is not fixed until you read it. When you encounter a **Superposition** node:
- Read carefully as multiple possibilities are presented.
- Click the **Collapse Action** button. This physical act represents your choice as an observer, forcing the wave function to collapse into a single thread of reality.

### 2. Monitoring Reality
Keep an eye on the **Quantum HUD** at the top:
- **STATE**: Indicates if the story is currently in flux or settled.
- **COHERENCE**: Represents how stable the current timeline is. A low coherence might lead to unexpected narrative glitches.

### 3. Collaborative Storytelling
You are more than a reader; you are a co-author of reality.
- **Observation Logs (Issues)**: If you notice a logical paradox or a compelling mystery, report it. The AI manager (QSM) monitors these logs to shape future chapters.
- **Timeline Forks (Pull Requests)**: Advanced observers can propose new story atoms or entire branches by submitting a Pull Request. If your timeline gains enough coherence, it becomes part of the official narrative.

### 4. Technical Requirements
A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox) is required to experience the full visual and narrative "collapse" effects.
